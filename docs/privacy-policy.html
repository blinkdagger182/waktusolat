Privacy Policy - Waktu

Effective date: February 26, 2026

Overview

Waktu is designed to be simple and privacy-focused. We minimize data collection and only use information needed to provide prayer time functionality.

Data We Use

  • Location data (optional): Used to calculate and show local prayer times and related notifications.
  • Notification preferences: Stored to send prayer reminders based on your settings.
  • App settings: Appearance and app preferences are stored on your device.

Donations & Purchases

In-app donation processing is handled by Apple and RevenueCat. We do not store your payment card details.

Third-Party Services

  • Waktu Solat API - prayer time data
  • Apple services - in-app purchases, notifications
  • RevenueCat - purchase management

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

Your Choices

  • You can disable location access anytime in iOS Settings.
  • You can disable notifications anytime in iOS Settings.
  • You can uninstall the app to remove local app data from your device.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact: support@riskcreatives.com
