Quick Troubleshooting

  • Prayer times not updating: Open the app once with internet enabled and location permission on.
  • Widget shows old data: Open Waktu, refresh prayer times, then re-add widget if needed.
  • No notifications: Enable notifications in iOS Settings and in Waktu notification settings.
  • Location issues: Set Location access to While Using App or Always.
